    XIAOFENG HUANG  黃小峰

    点击即可编辑文本

    Xiaofeng Huang is an art historian, the Deputy Dean and Associate Professor of the School of Humanities at the Central Academy of Arts, Beijing. His major research field is history of Chinese paintings, history of Chinese paintings and calligraphy collections, dating and authentification of classical Chinese paintings and calligraphy. He was also the year 2013/2014 Visiting Scholar of the Art History Program at the Harvard-Yenching Institute, and the year 2013/2014 Fellow of the J. S. Lee Memorial Fellowship Program initiated by the Bei Shan Tang Foundation. His publications include New Eyes on Old Pictures: How to Look at Chinese Paintings (2021), Elegant Gathering in the West Garden: a Guide to Chinese Figure Painting - Vol. 5 [Song Dynasty] (2010), etc. He has published several research papers in key academic journals, and has been a columnist of Chinese Heritage. 


    黃小峰，藝術史學者，現為中央美術學院教授、人文學院副院長。主要研究方向為中國繪畫史、古書畫鑒定學、書畫鑒藏史。曾為哈佛燕京學社「藝術史項目」2010-2011年度訪問學者、香港北山堂基金「利榮森紀念交流計劃」2013-2014年度訪問學人。著有《古画新品录》（2021年）、《西園雅集：中國人物畫通鑒5》[兩宋時期]（2010年）等。他的多篇學術論文亦於多本重要學術刊物發表，並為《中華遺產》雜誌專欄作者。

    HUGH MOSS  莫士撝

    点击即可编辑文本

    Hugh Moss, also known by his studio name “The Master of the Water, Pine and Stone Retreat”, born Wimbledon, son of one of London's major Chinese art dealers. Brought up with, and spent a lifetime studying, collecting, dealing in, and writing about Chinese art and culture and then becoming a practitioner of Chinese painting. Published art theorist, with also an enormous bibliography of books articles and lectures on Chinese art. His publications include: Art Reboot (2022), Beyond the Stage of Time: The Master of the Water, Pine and Stone Retreat (2020), The Art of Understanding Art: a New Perspective (2016), etc. 


    莫士撝，齋號水松石山房主人，生於英國溫布頓，其父為經營中國藝術的倫敦大古董商。他自幼受家庭熏陶，其後繼承父親衣缽。一生致力於中國文化藝術的研究、鑒藏與撰寫，並成為中國畫的創作實踐者。作為一個藝術理論家，他出版了大量有關中國藝術的書籍、文章，並就相關話題作了大量的公開講演。他的著作有《欣賞藝術的藝術：一個全新的視角》（2016年）， 《中國鼻煙壺藝術》（1993年），以及《文玩萃珍》（1986年）等。

    Founded in 2011, the Fanzhi Foundation for Art and Education is a registered non-profit organisation that supports the development of modern and contemporary art by organising public seminars, exhibitions and research programmes. 


    The Fanzhi Foundation launched Art Talks in 2017. Topics evoking profound influence and inspiration in art history are selected. Local and overseas leading scholars are invited to introduce their innovative theories and methodologies, with a goal to encourage exchanges and discussion among young scholars in Asia, hence cultivate a broader research perspective. 


    Over the past years have scholars from mainland China, U.K., the Netherlands and U.S.A. been invited to Art Talks. Seminars are open to the public for free; video transcribed in bilingual languages for free access in public domain.

    梵志藝術與教育基金會，是在2011年在香港注冊（政府注冊編號1668142），藝術與教育類的非營利機構，持續致力於支持美術館展覽及舉辦免費講座。 


    「梵藝 × 講座：藝術史專題講座」是基金會于2017年啟動之項目，選定藝術史學科中影響深遠、啟發深思的議題，邀請海內外該領域的翹楚學者，講述嶄新的理論、觀點以及方法論，鼓勵亞洲區青年學人，參與交流與討論，以期孕育更寬廣的研究視野。 


    過去幾年，邀請了中國、英國、荷蘭、美國的學人，講論關於繪畫藝術的不同課題。講座視頻配以中、英雙語字幕及資料配圖，於社交平台，向公眾及教育界免費開放瀏覽。
